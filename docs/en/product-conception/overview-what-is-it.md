# Overview — What Is It?

This document describes what Intensity is, who it is for, and the value it proposes. It is written for anyone who needs to understand the product without technical knowledge — users, stakeholders, managers, investors, and product analysts.

---

## Short

**Intensity** is a mobile app that helps couples and friend groups create deeper connection through shared experiences. Instead of repeating the same routines or waiting for the perfect moment, people collect unusual ideas over time in themed **boxes**, rate how intense each idea feels, and when they are together they **draw** one at random and **reveal** it deliberately. The product turns postponed intentions into lived moments — with presence, not as a checklist.

---

## Medium

Intensity addresses a familiar gap: relationships that feel stuck in repetitive experiences, and memorable moments that are usually the most unexpected ones — yet almost always get postponed.

The app offers a simple rhythm: **collect ideas → draw one → live a memorable moment**. Users organize ideas in **boxes** tailored to different contexts (outings as a couple, trips with friends, breaking routine, and others). Each person can contribute ideas on their own; when the group meets, they enter together, draw from the box, align on mood and limits, and only then reveal the full experience.

Groups form when people play together and can grow when members **invite** others to join. Intensity speaks directly to people who miss closeness and want to act rather than wait for chance. It is not positioned as a task manager or social network; its essence is **connection, intensity, and discovery** — living meaningful moments with presence.

Two complementary ways of using the app shape the experience: **Experiences** (individual login to register ideas) and **Experience Box** (group login to create boxes, manage them, and live the draw together).

## Detailed

### The problem and the opportunity

The onboarding narrative frames a problem many people recognize: tiredness with **repetitive experiences** that barely bring them closer to the people who matter, and a longing for **deeper connection**. It observes that the most memorable moments were often the most **unusual** — the different experiences that truly brought people together — and yet those experiences are **almost always postponed**, waiting for the perfect opportunity that may never come.

Intensity positions itself as an answer to that inertia: **do not wait for chance to create those moments**. The product encourages action through a structured but playful mechanism — collecting unusual ideas and taking a random draw seriously when the group is ready.

### What the product is

At its core, Intensity is a **shared experience bank** for small groups — couples or circles of friends. Each group builds a collection of concrete ideas ("experiences") organized in **boxes** with names and themes. Over time, members feed the boxes with suggestions. When together, they activate a **draw moment**: one experience is selected, its intensity and parameters are shown first, the group aligns, and then the card is flipped to reveal the full description.

The product is delivered as a **mobile application**.

### Who it is for

The language and box themes point clearly to **couples** and **friend groups** who want to:

- Break out of routine
- Try new things together, including first times and light discomfort
- Deepen intimacy and connection
- Discover experiences they would not normally propose

The app does not define age ranges, geographic markets, or formal user personas. The audience is inferred from how the product speaks and what kinds of boxes it offers.

### Value proposition

| Dimension | What Intensity offers |
|-----------|----------------------|
| **Connection** | Shared rituals that bring people closer, not just shared activities |
| **Discovery** | A space to propose unusual ideas safely, with reflection before adding them |
| **Action** | A mechanism (collect + draw) that replaces endless postponement |
| **Structure** | Boxes by theme, intensity awareness, and a deliberate reveal ritual |
| **Presence** | Emphasis on living the moment — not completing a task |
| **Inclusion** | Groups can grow through invitations without forcing everyone to log in at once |

### Positioning and essence

Intensity distinguishes itself from generic idea lists or calendar apps by combining **asynchronous contribution** (each person adds ideas alone) with **synchronous revelation** (the draw happens when everyone is together). Randomness is treated as a **commitment device**, not a game — the quick guide instructs users to take the draw seriously.

The product's stated essence is: **connection, intensity, and discovery**. It explicitly rejects a task-completion framing: *"It's not about completing a task: it's about living meaningful moments with presence."*

### How the product presents itself

- **Brand name:** Intensity
- **Key terms users see:** Experience, Box, Experience Box, Group, Intensity, Draw, Reveal, Seal, Invite
- **Tone:** Direct, encouraging, intimate — speaks to feelings of closeness and courage
