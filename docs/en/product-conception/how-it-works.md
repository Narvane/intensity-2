# How It Works

This document explains how Intensity works from the user's perspective — the general rhythm, the two access modes, and the macro-flows that shape daily use. It is written for stakeholders, designers, and anyone who needs to understand the product without technical detail.

---

## Short

Intensity revolves around a repeating cycle: **collect ideas alone → meet as a group → draw one experience → align → reveal → live the moment**. Users contribute ideas asynchronously in **Experiences** mode; they create boxes, invite members, and run the draw ritual together in **Experience Box** mode on a shared phone. Each experience carries an intensity level and parameters shown before the full description is revealed.

---

## Medium

### The central rhythm

Every meaningful use of Intensity follows the same arc:

```
Contribute (alone) → Gather as a group → Draw → Align → Reveal → Live the moment
```

The cycle repeats as new ideas are added over days or weeks.

### Two complementary modes

| | **Experiences** | **Experience Box** |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Who** | One person | Whole group, together |
| **When** | Async, over time | Sync, when everyone is present |
| **Purpose** | Register experiences; pick group and box | Create and manage boxes; invite members; run draw and reveal |
| **Device** | Each person's own phone | One shared phone for the ritual |

**Experiences** lets a **proponent** choose a group and box, then add ideas through a guided five-step assistant. **Experience Box** lets the group authenticate together, see their boxes, create new ones, invite people who are not present, delete boxes when appropriate, and run the draw ritual.

### Forming and growing a group

A **group** is the set of people who share boxes. It is born in two ways:

1. **Joint login** — when two or more people enter Experience Box mode together, their combination defines (or reopens) a group.
2. **Invitation** — any member can share an invite link or code so someone else joins the group without being present at login.

Once in a group, a member can contribute ideas from their own phone in Experiences mode and participate in rituals when the group meets.

### The draw ritual (macro view)

When the group opens a box together:

1. They optionally filter by intensity (any, exact level, or up to a level).
2. They **draw** — one experience is chosen at random from eligible ideas.
3. The card shows **intensity and parameters only** — not the full text.
4. The group **aligns** on mood, limits, and commitment before flipping the card.
5. They **reveal** the full description and the proponent's reflection.
6. If it does not fit the moment, they can **return to the draw**.

### Creating an experience (macro view)

The five-step assistant guides contributors through: suggestion → reflection → parameters → intensity classification → save (with optional variations). Experiences are stored in the chosen box and become available for future draws.

## Detailed

### Collection phase — Experiences mode

After individual login, the proponent selects a **group** (from groups they belong to) and a **box** within that group. The experience list shows their contributions to the active box.

Creating a new experience follows five illustrative steps:

| Step | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Suggestion** | Describe the idea or tap themed examples for inspiration |
| **Reflection** | Justify why the group would accept it — empathy and consent |
| **Parameters** | Rate effort, openness, and innovativeness (each 1–5) |
| **Classification** | Confirm or adjust overall intensity (suggested from parameters) |
| **Branching** | Review summary; save and create another, or finish |

The app warns that experiences are **not encrypted** — transparency is part of the social contract.

### Live ritual — Experience Box mode

After multi-user login on a shared device, the group sees **Box Home** — their boxes, with options to create a box, **invite** a member, or **delete** a box (with confirmation). Opening a box enters the **shared moment**:

- Filter chips: **Any** / **Exact** / **Up to** intensity
- **Draw** selects randomly among eligible experiences
- Result card shows cover (intensity, parameters, integrity seal) — text hidden
- Amber alignment hint prompts group agreement before reveal
- **Reveal** flips the card to show full description and reflection
- **Back to draw** starts a new selection if the moment is not right

### Invitations (macro view)

Any group member can generate an invite from Box Home or from group context in Experiences mode. The invite offers a **shareable link** and a **short code**. The recipient previews who is in the group and accepts to join. Invites expire after seven days; the inviter can revoke an active invite.

### Recommended social rhythm (optional, not enforced)

- Each person feeds boxes over time from their own phone
- When together: activate the draw on one shared phone
- Before reveal: align on climate, limits, commitment
- Optional practices: real-cost consequences if someone backs out; swapping experiences of different intensities; gradual intensity progression over time

These social practices are guidance in the quick guide — the app does not track or enforce them.

### What happens between sessions

Ideas contributed individually accumulate in the group's boxes. When the group meets again and logs in together (or a subset with remaining members), the pool is refreshed from the server before drawing. Draw results are not stored — each activation is a fresh moment.
